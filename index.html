Our Services

Complete residential and commercial HVAC services throughout Orange County and Riverside County.

Air Conditioning

Repair, replacement, and tune-up services for all makes and models. Keep your home cool all summer long.

Furnace & Heating

Full furnace repair, replacement, and safety inspections. We keep your heating system running efficiently and safely.

New System Installation

Complete HVAC installations including furnace, coil, AC unit, gas, electrical, ducting, thermostat, and all accessories.

Indoor Air Quality

Media filters, HEPA filters, electronic air cleaners, UV lights, humidifiers, and whole house fans for cleaner air.

Ductwork

Ductwork repair, replacement, asbestos abatement, and reconfiguration for remodeling projects.

Commercial HVAC

Split system installation, packaged gas/electric and heat pump systems, servicing, and maintenance contracts for commercial properties.

Family-Owned Since 1997

Seaside Air Conditioning and Heating has been serving South Orange County for over 27 years. Founded and led by President Victor Serrao, we are a family-owned and operated business headquartered in San Juan Capistrano, CA.

Our commitment is simple: honesty, professionalism, and value-added service. We take the time to understand your needs and resolve your HVAC problems while maximizing your comfort — guaranteed.

We are proud to be the exclusive HVAC service provider to True Value Hardware stores in our geographic area, and Angie's List Super Service Award winners.
